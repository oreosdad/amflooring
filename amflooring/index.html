About A&M Flooring

Flooring Experts
You Can Trust

A&M Flooring, LLC is dedicated to providing reliable, dependable, professional and trustworthy sales and installation on all of our products. We are a local, family owned company that has been serving the Kansas City metro for over 30 years.

We specialize in any and all flooring projects — commercial and residential. We can handle any size project and can work multiple installation crews per project if needed. Our full showroom is open to the public in the heart of Johnson County, 6 days a week.

A&M Flooring has built relationships with flooring manufacturers for over 30 years. We will match or beat any competitor's prices and offer 0% financing options.
